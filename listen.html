Biography

Supa Sax hails from Harlem, a neighbourhood in Upper Manhattan, New York City. After exposure to some of the world's greatest jazz musicians at the Manhattan school of music Supa Sax embarked on a mission to defy genres. Blending jazz, folk, hip-hop, african, latin feels creates that signature SS sound. It has been said that if you drive across the Brooklyn bridge with the windows down at night you are sure to hear him practicing.
